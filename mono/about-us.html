We are a global 3D graphic
production house based in Vietnam.
The only rule is that there are no rules. Our environment encourages a free interchange of ideas, which leads to more creativity with form, [...] that revolutionise attitudes to graphic design.
As suggested by the name, "Mono" , one, signifies that every single one of us performs as a vital component of a body. "Station" refers to how more people board the train at each stop. It is implied that designers could act as travelers. We all travel on the same train, have the same driving forces and ultimate objectives, and will arrive at the same place.
