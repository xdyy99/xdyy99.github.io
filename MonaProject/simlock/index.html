How can I find IMEI?

  • On SIM card tray

    For find imei, you need remove sim card tray from device, and then you can find imei on it. (not on all models you can find imeu via sim tray)

  • In setting

    Go to Settings > General > About and look for your device IMEI

  • Dial *#06# to get IMEI

    Before trying anything else, you can try simply dialing *#06# on your iPhone (or any mobile phone), this should retrieve the IMEI numbers. Give it a moment and the IMEI will appear on the screen this little number dial trick actually works to retrieve the IMEI on other cell phones too, not just iPhone
